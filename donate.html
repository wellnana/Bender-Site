Donate to Bender
Here are some ways you can donate to Bender's development.
Join the support server!
Patreon
Make a monthly contribution to provide continuous support and keep Bender running smoothly!
For $1/month or more, you will receive the @Supporter role, and will have access to a channel to chat with other Supporters and staff!
For $5/month or more, you will recieve the @Sponsor role, access to a special channel for Sponsors and staff to suggest features, as well as your name mentioned on the homepage as a thank-you. You may also request a custom role on Discord!
Paypal
Make a one-time contribution to Bender's creator to help develop the bot.
For $15 or more, you will receive the @Supporter role, and will have access to a channel to chat with other Supporters and staff!
For $30 or more, you will recieve the @Sponsor role, access to a special channel for Sponsors and staff to suggest features, as well as your name mentioned on the homepage as a thank-you. You may also request a custom role on Discord!
