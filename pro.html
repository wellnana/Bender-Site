Bender Pro!?
Bender Pro enables many new features, including:
  • - ;backup and ;restore ~ Create backups of your server and restore them if something goes wrong.
  • - ;news ~ Show news for a game, or configure it to automatically be posted in a certain channel.
  • - ;roleall ~ Add, remove, or toggle roles for all server members at once.
  • - ;nickall ~ Set or reset nicknames of all server members.
  • - ;volume ~ Change volume at which Bender plays music.
  • - You can play songs up to 24 hours long rather than the normal limit of two hours.
  • - You can have up to 7 autoroles rather than just one.
  • - Image manipulation commands: ;blur, ;brightness, ;contrast, ;deepfry, ;graycale, ;invert, ;jpeg, ;mirror, ;pulse, ;rotate, ;saturate, ;sepia, ;shake, and ;spin
  • - Meme creation commands: ;bobross, ;brain, ;changemymind, ;fuckedup, ;gay, ;gaysingles, ;killit, ;spiderman, and ;steppedinshit
  • - More Bender Pro features are planned, such as embeds for welcome messages and the ability to add groups of joinable roles.
  • - If you have an idea for a new feature for Bender Pro, feel free to type it in #suggestions in Bender's Lair.

Once you link your Discord account, you will also receive the @Bender Pro User role in Bender's Lair.
