What does Bender do for my server?

Bender is a high-quality bot packed with many features (over 180 commands!) To name a few:

  • A customizable agreement system to require users to accept the rules/terms before joining your server.
  • Moderation and automod commands to keep your server clear of spammers and other undesirable people.
  • Game stats commands for many popular games, including Apex Legends, CSGO, PUBG, Battlefield V, and more.
  • Configurable permissions system so you can choose the exact level of access you want for each command.
  • NSFW commands that can fetch a variety of images, GIFs, and videos.
  • Image editing commands & meme creation commands (available with Bender Pro.)


To see more info about Bender's features, see the commands page.
